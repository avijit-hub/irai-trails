ABOUT IRAITRAILS

Whether you’re planning a thrilling safari expedition to the famous Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve or simply want to relax in nature’s lap, Irai Trails offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern amenities. Guests can unwind in the swimming pool, enjoy evenings at the bar counter, and make use of fully equipped kitchen facilities for a personalized culinary experience. On request, the homestay also provides catering services tailored to guest preferences. Surrounded by dense forest and the soothing flow of the river, Irai Trails offers not just accommodation—but an immersive nature experience. Wake up to the call of birds, enjoy a cup of tea with a jungle view, and explore the untamed beauty of Tadoba just minutes away.
